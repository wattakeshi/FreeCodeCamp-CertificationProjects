How it Works

The process begins with an initial conversation between the coach and the client. This is a crucial step for establishing a clear understanding of your current training level and your personal goals.

During this discussion, we'll talk about your fitness history, any past injuries, your experience with different types of exercise, and what you hope to achieve. This information helps me tailor a workout plan that's not only effective but also safe and sustainable for you.

Once we've identified your stage, we will build a plan that you're excited about and can stick with, ensuring a successful and rewarding fitness journey.
